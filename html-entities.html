HTML Entities Encoder/Decoder

Encode special characters to HTML entities or decode HTML entities back to text.

Examples

Encoding:

Hello & "World" <script>

→ Hello & "World" <script>

Decoding:

&lt;div&gt;Content&lt;/div&gt;

→ <div>Content</div>
